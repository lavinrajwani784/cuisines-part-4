Population and Cities

Japan has a population of nearly 123 million, making it the 11th most populous country in the world . Tokyo, the capital, is one of the largest metropolitan areas globally and serves as the political, economic, and cultural center . Other major cities include Osaka, Kyoto, and Yokohama.

Language and Culture

The official language is Japanese, which uses kanji characters along with hiragana and katakana scripts . Japanese culture emphasizes respect, family, and community, with traditions such as visiting gardens for meditation and practicing Shintō and Buddhist rituals . Japanese cuisine is rice- and fish-based, low in fat, and contributes to the country’s high life expectancy
